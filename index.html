Instructions

1. Upload an image of your face or use the camera to take a face picture. Ensure the photo is close to the eyes, and avoid wearing glasses.

2. Submit the image to analyze and detect any potential eye diseases for both eyes.

3. The results for the left and right eyes will be displayed.

Tip: Use Computer for better performance. In mobiles, some features may not work.

Note:

After submitting the request, please wait a moment for the result viewing windows to load completely.

Eye Disease Detection System (EDDS)

This application uses Machine Learning (ML) to detect common eye diseases from images of the eyes.

Detectable Diseases

  • Cataracts
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Corneal Ulcer
  • Ectropion
